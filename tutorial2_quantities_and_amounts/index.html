NORTH AMERICAN NISSAN SKYLINE GTR SALES

"The demise of Godzilla"

Synopsis

The Nissan GT-R is a high-performance sports car and grand tourer produced by Nissan that was unveiled in 2007. It is the successor to the Skyline GT-R, although no longer part of the Skyline range itself, that name now being used for Nissan's luxury-sport market. Nissan's most performance oriented vehicle the GT-R, had only sold 331 units respectively, down from 538 of the entirety of 2018, resulting in a -38.5% figure.

This may be due to market anticipation of a new model and a more focused group of buyers.
