Activated Partial Thromboplastin Time (aPTT)

Patient Care / Service Suggestive Correlation Scatterplot

Synopsis

Activated Partial Thromboplastin Time (aPTT) is a screening test that helps evaluate a person's ability to appropriately form blood clots. This scatterplot graph represents clinical and logistical dataset of aPTT specimens received from an Outpatient business line. Depending on specified baselines, indicators may suggest potential logistical deficiencies and / or additional sensitivity of clinical abnormal values that would require additional monitoring of these specimens as part of the intake process. Based on the 240 min time processing expectation from time where patient was drawn to time test has been processed by a clinical instrument, any aPTT processed after 240 mins is not meeting service goals and may potentially impact quality of clinical results and patient care. White means exceeding expectations, Yellow means meeting expectations, and Jade means failing expectations.
